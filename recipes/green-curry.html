Green Curry

A gold standard Thai dish. Extra spicy and delicious when made with fresh chicken and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 50g green curry paste
  • 360ml of coconut milk
  • 200g protein of choice (chicken recommended)
  • 200g Chinese or Japanese eggplant
  • 1 small can of bamboo shoots
  • 1 can of quail eggs
  • 1.5 tsp sugar
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Stir-fry 50g green curry paste in 1 tbsp cooking oil, then add 200ml coconut milk.
  2. Add 200g protein and continue cooking.
  3. Add another 120ml coconut milk and 120ml (0.5 cup) water and bring to boiling.
  4. Turn heat slightly down and add eggplant, bamboo shoots and quail eggs, continue cooking until eggplant is to preference.
  5. Add 1.5 tsp sugar, mix and serve hot.
